About

I'm Tom Easterbrook. I've spent my working life around adult social care and local government — the parts that rarely make the headlines but shape people's lives every single day.

This blog is where I write it down: the good days, the hard ones, the systems that work and the ones that need fixing. I write for the people doing the work, the people receiving care, and anyone curious about how it all actually fits together.
