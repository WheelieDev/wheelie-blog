About
the author

I've spent my working life around adult social care and local government — the parts that rarely make the headlines but quietly shape people's lives every single day.

I also move through those same systems from a wheelchair, which means I tend to see them from both sides of the desk. That double vision — part insider, part the person on the receiving end — is really what this blog is about.

“The good days, the hard ones, the systems that work and the ones that badly need fixing.”

So I write it down — for the people doing the work, the people receiving care, and anyone curious about how it all actually fits together. No jargon, no spin. Just an honest view from where I sit. Thanks for being here.
